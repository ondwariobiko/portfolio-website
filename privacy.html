Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Your privacy is important to me. This policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

I believe in minimal data collection. Here's what I gather when you interact with my website:

  • Direct Contact: When you email me at ondwariobiko@gmail.com or use my contact form
  • Website Usage: Basic analytics through cookies to improve your experience
  • Service Inquiries: Only what's necessary to respond to your design requests

2. Use of Information

Every piece of information has a clear purpose:

  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and discuss potential projects
  • Improvement: Analyzing website usage to enhance your browsing experience
  • Updates: Occasional newsletters (only if you explicitly opt-in)

I never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Protection

Your data's security is my priority. I implement:

  • Encryption: Secure HTTPS connections throughout the website
  • Access Control: Strict limitations on who can access your information
  • Regular Audits: Keeping systems updated against vulnerabilities
  • Minimal Retention: Only keeping data as long as necessary

4. Third-Party Services

For optimal functionality, I use these trusted services:

  • Google Analytics: Helps understand how visitors use my site
  • Social Platforms: Behance integration to showcase my work
  • Email Services: For professional communication

Each service maintains its own privacy standards. I carefully select partners who respect user privacy.

5. Your Rights

You're in control of your data. You can:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Ask for corrections or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities

6. Policy Updates

This policy may evolve to reflect changes in regulations or my services. Significant updates will be clearly communicated.

I recommend checking this page periodically for the latest information.

Questions About Your Privacy?

I'm happy to discuss any concerns or requests regarding your personal data.

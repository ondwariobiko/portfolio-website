Hello! I’m John Obiko,a passionate freelance graphic and web designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of color theory and typography, I specialize in creating visually stunning designs that effectively communicate messages to target audiences.

I offer a wide range of services, from logo design to marketing materials, each project is a unique opportunity for me to help businesses and individuals create a strong visual identity. My designs are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional, ensuring that they serve their intended purpose effectively. My goal is to help my clients communicate their unique brand stories through compelling visuals. I believe that good design is not just about looking good – it’s about creating an experience that feels right and works well.

If you’re looking for a dedicated, creative, and experienced graphic designer to bring your vision to life, let’s connect! I’m excited to learn about your project and how I can help you create a design that tells your story.
